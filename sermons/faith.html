Speaker: Sis Biodun

Biblical Reading: Exodus 34:6

Date: Nov 9, 2025

Mercy is the heartbeat of God's love a love that seeks to forgive and uplift.

God mercy is not a weakness but a reflection of boundless love for us .He does not treat us as our sins deserve but lavishes us with compassion and grace

The greatest act of God's mercy to us is sending his son to die for us.
