About Breakthrough Parish

Breakthrough parish is a vibrant community of believers commited to spreadind love and power of jesus Christ. Our mission is to help individuals experience a personal relationship with God, discover their lives through the word and the Holy Spirit.

At Breakthrough parish we believe in faith, prayer, and fellowship. we are passionate about empowering families, transforming communities, and nuturing the next generation of leaders who will impact the world of christ. Whether you are new to the faith or seeking a deeper walk with God, you are welcome here - a place where lives are restored and destinies are fulfilled

Join us every sunday for a refreshing time of worship, inspiring teachings, and an atmosphere filled with love, hope, and breakthrough. Together, we are building a strong, spirit-filled family of faith in lagos, Nigeria.
